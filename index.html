Your Orientation Guide

Dining Halls & Cafes

Welcome to Starlight University's dining experience! Our main dining hall, The Comet Cafe, offers a wide variety of options from grill items to a fresh salad bar. For a quick bite, visit the Astro Lounge in the library for coffee and pastries. All locations accept the student meal plan.

Key Campus Buildings

The heart of our campus is the Orion Library, open 24/5 during the semester. The Nebula Science Center houses all our STEM labs and is located on the west side of campus. For arts and humanities, the Pegasus Hall is your destination, right next to the main quad.

Campus Life & Events

Get involved! Our Student Union hosts over 100 clubs. Check the university calendar for weekly events like movie nights on the lawn and guest speakers. The annual Starlight Festival in the fall is a can't-miss event with music, food, and games.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Orion Library is open 24 hours from Sunday at noon until Friday at 8 PM. It is open from 10 AM to 6 PM on Saturdays.
The IT Help Desk is located on the first floor of the Orion Library. You can also submit a ticket online through the university portal.
Visit the Student Activities Fair at the beginning of each semester, or browse the list of clubs on the Student Union website and contact the club leaders directly.
